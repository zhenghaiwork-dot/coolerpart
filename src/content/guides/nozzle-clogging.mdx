## Common Causes

- Mineral scale buildup from hard water
- Debris or dirt in water supply
- Algae growth in water tank
- Deteriorated nozzle material

## How to Fix

### 1. Clean the Nozzle

Remove the nozzle from the distribution pipe. Soak in white vinegar or descaling solution for 30 minutes. Use a fine needle to clear any remaining blockages.

### 2. Clean the Water System

Drain and clean the water tank. Check and clean the water strainer/filter. Inspect the pump inlet for debris.

### 3. Replace if Needed

If cleaning doesn't restore spray pattern or if the nozzle shows signs of deterioration, replace with a new spray nozzle.

### 4. Prevention

Install a water strainer before the distribution system. Use water treatment tablets. Schedule quarterly cleaning and inspection.
